Diagnostic Analytics

The two major approaches that have been identified for diagnostic analysis are:

  1. assessment of deficiency, which focuses on student's weaknesses
  2. analysis of errors, which focuses on the types of errors the student makes

The purpose of the diagnostic analysis is not only to identify the strengths and the weakneasses, but also to investigate why the user has these weak points (perhaps a lack of information, a lack of practice or it is an isolated event, produced only once, caused by the lack of attention).
